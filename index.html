Why do you smoke cigars?

I smoke cigars as a form of meditation. It allows me to sit and be with my thoughts. It's hard to just sit in peace these days while trying to run around this crazy world. Cigars have given me a reason to do just that sit and take it all in.

My Humidor

My humidor contains the cigars I enjoy, and some that I have wanted to try. Every ones tastes vary. Some people like flavored cigars, some like sweet cigars, and others like what I call normal cigars. The key is finding what you like.

One thing I've learned on my cigar journey is don't worry about what anyone else says. If you like what you smoke then that is all that matters. You are smoking it not them.

My favorite saying I follow "Any day is a good day to smoke a good cigar"! Find what your "Good Cigar" is and enjoy.
